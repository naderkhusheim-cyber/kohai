What she does

🌸 Lives on your terminal

Pinned to the bottom-right of whatever terminal is frontmost — Terminal.app, iTerm2, Ghostty, WezTerm, Warp. Click-through so your typing isn't blocked.

🎭 Multiple personalities

Kohai (default), Girlfriend (clingy + jealous), Coach (hype), Maid (formal). Switch with /kohai-personality <name>.

🧠 Claude-driven scenes

Tell her anything via /kohai-do — "sit at your desk", "wave hi", "point at line 42", "drink water". Claude composes the scene live; no canned animations.

💧 Reminders that aren't text

She holds the water bottle and tilts it to drink, picks up a snack when it's late, dims the lights at 1am. Reminders feel like company, not popups.

🪄 Reacts to your work

Tests pass → she celebrates. Build fails → she leans toward the screen looking worried. Long silence → she walks across the screen to check on you.
